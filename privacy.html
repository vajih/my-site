Privacy Policy

Your privacy, in plain English

We collect only what we need to help you find official manufacturer savings and to respond when you contact us. We don’t sell your personal data.

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Summary

  • We link you to official manufacturer programs and provide educational guidance.
  • We collect minimal data (e.g., email if you ask us to send a link or subscribe).
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • You can request access or deletion at any time (see Your privacy choices).

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide (e.g., email, name) when you use our forms.
  • Context you provide in a message (e.g., drug name, insurer type).
  • Technical basics: IP address, device/browser information, and referrer (used for security, analytics, and abuse prevention).
  • Usage data: pages or links you interact with on SaveRx.ai (to improve our site).

We do not collect health records. Do not share protected health information (PHI) beyond what is necessary to ask your question.

How we use your information

  • To send you the official manufacturer link or respond to your message.
  • To operate, secure, and improve SaveRx.ai.
  • To prevent spam/abuse and maintain service integrity.
  • With your opt-in, to send updates such as a newsletter (you can unsubscribe anytime).

How we share information

We don’t sell your data. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, email delivery, analytics) under contract to process data on our behalf.
  • Legal reasons (e.g., comply with law, protect rights, prevent fraud).

Cookies & analytics

We use lightweight analytics and essential cookies to keep the site reliable, measure usage, and detect abuse. You can control cookies via your browser settings. We avoid intrusive tracking.

Data retention & security

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purpose we collected it, unless a longer retention period is required by law. We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data; no method is 100% secure.

Your privacy choices

  • Access/Deletion: Request a copy or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails via the link in any message.
  • Corrections: Ask us to fix inaccurate information.
  • Do Not Sell/Share: We don’t sell your data; you can still submit a request below.

Use the form below or email privacy@saverx.ai.

Submit a privacy request

We will verify your request and respond as required by applicable law.

Children’s privacy

SaveRx.ai is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we’ll remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We’ll change the “Last updated” date above and, when appropriate, provide additional notice.
