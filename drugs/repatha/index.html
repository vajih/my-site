FAQ

Is SaveRx enrolling me automatically?

No. By program Terms, enrollment must be completed by the patient (or legal representative) on Amgen’s official website. SaveRx simply helps you copy your details to save time.

Does SaveRx store my information?

SaveRx stores only the information you already provided to us. The assist page shows it back to you with convenient “Copy” buttons. We do not auto-submit to Amgen, and this public page does not collect any data.

What if my link doesn’t work?

Personal links are secure and can expire when refreshed. Ask SaveRx support to resend a new link, or go directly to Amgen’s enrollment and complete it manually.
