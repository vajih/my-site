STELARA®

Proven biologic for immune-mediated conditions

STELARA® (ustekinumab) is a prescription biologic that targets IL-12 and IL-23. It’s used for adults with moderate to severe plaque psoriasis, active psoriatic arthritis, Crohn’s disease, and ulcerative colitis.
