Common questions

What is a manufacturer copay card?

A copay card is an official savings program from the drug’s manufacturer that can lower your monthly cost if you’re eligible (usually for those with commercial insurance). We send you directly to that program.

Do I need to create an account on SaveRx.ai?

No. We don’t run the savings program—manufacturers do. We’ll ask for your email so we can pass you through and share the exact link, then you enroll with the manufacturer.

Will this work if I’m uninsured or on Medicare?

Eligibility varies by program. Many copay cards exclude government insurance. Some brands offer separate patient assistance programs—look for that option on the manufacturer’s page.
