Lowering out-of-pocket costs—clearly and quickly

SaveRx.ai helps you find and use official manufacturer savings programs for leading medications. We show the exact steps to enroll and what to expect at the pharmacy.

What we do

  • Curate current, official savings links (copay cards, patient support, assistance).
  • Explain eligibility in plain language and outline the steps to enroll.
  • Help patients, caregivers, and clinics save time and reduce costs.

What we are (and aren’t)

We are a free guidance service. We are not a pharmacy, PBM, insurance broker, or prescriber.

We don’t sell data. We collect only what’s needed to assist you, with your consent.

How it works

  1. Select your medication.
  2. We link you to the manufacturer’s official program page.
  3. Follow our quick checklist to enroll and use the savings at the pharmacy.

Eligibility varies by drug, insurance, and location. Always read the official program terms.

For patients & caregivers

Commercial insurance, no insurance, or unsure—we point you to the best next step without jargon.

For clinicians & office staff

Use our pages during benefits conversations to route patients to the correct enrollment quickly.

Partnerships: PBMs, pharmacies, and manufacturers
