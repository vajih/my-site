About SaveRx

We believe everyone deserves transparent and affordable healthcare.

Our mission is to connect you with official manufacturer savings programs and walk you through enrollment, so you can use them at the pharmacy with ease — no gimmicks, no runaround.

Explore medications Partner with us

Our story

This started with something personal. I was diagnosed with high cholesterol and prescribed Repatha. At the pharmacy counter, the price shocked me. Over weeks of calls, forms, and trial-and-error, I finally found the official copay card—and it brought the cost down dramatically. But it shouldn’t have been that hard.

Standing in line, I watched people—especially older patients—pay full price because they didn’t know where to look. Pharmacists would say, “Yes, there are coupon cards, but it’s hard to get to that lower price.” When you just want to be healthy, you’ll pay almost anything in that moment. I wanted to fix that.

With encouragement from my children, I put up this website so anyone prescribed these medications can quickly find the real, official savings and understand how to use them. If it helped me with Repatha, it can help others with Repatha and many other drugs.

— Founder, SaveRx.ai

What we do

  • Curate up-to-date links to manufacturer savings (copay cards, support, and assistance).
  • Explain eligibility in plain language and outline the exact steps to enroll.
  • Help patients, caregivers, and clinics save time and reduce costs at checkout.

What we are (and aren’t)

We are a free guidance service. We are not a pharmacy, PBM, insurer, or prescriber.

We don’t sell data. We collect only what’s needed to assist you, with your consent.

How it works

  1. Select your medication.
  2. We link you to the manufacturer’s official program page.
  3. Follow our short checklist to enroll and use the card at the pharmacy.

Eligibility varies by drug, insurance, and location. Always review the official terms.

For patients & caregivers

Commercial insurance, no insurance, or unsure—we’ll guide you to the best next step without jargon.

For clinicians & office staff

Use our pages during benefits conversations to route patients to correct enrollments—fast.

Partnerships: PBMs, pharmacies, and manufacturers

We’re aligning with payers and supply-chain partners to make affordability simple and compliant. If you represent a PBM, a pharmacy chain (national or regional), or a pharmaceutical manufacturer, let’s collaborate.

  • PBMs: education flows, routing, and eligibility clarity to reduce friction and abandon rates.
  • Pharmacies: at-counter guidance, BIN/PCN/Group tips, and patient-friendly instructions.
  • Manufacturers: accurate program representation, enrollment uplift, and real-world feedback loops.
