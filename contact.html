Partnerships with PBMs, pharmacies, and pharmaceutical companies

We collaborate with PBMs, retail/independent pharmacies, and manufacturers to improve affordability and reduce confusion at checkout. Let’s co-design compliant, patient-friendly flows that increase program utilization and satisfaction.

  • PBMs: education and routing that reduce abandon rates and support formulary strategy.
  • Pharmacies: clear use of BIN/PCN/Group details and at-counter guidance to speed transactions.
  • Manufacturers: accurate program representation, enrollment uplift, and actionable feedback loops.
