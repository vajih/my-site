Partner with us (PBMs, pharmacies, manufacturers)

SaveRx.ai connects patients with official savings and assistance programs—clearly and compliantly. We collaborate with PBMs, retail/independent pharmacies, and manufacturers to reduce confusion at checkout and increase program utilization.

  • PBMs: education and routing that support formulary strategy and reduce abandon rates.
  • Pharmacies: clear BIN/PCN/Group usage and counter-friendly guidance to speed transactions.
  • Manufacturers: accurate program representation, uplift in enrollments, and actionable feedback loops.
