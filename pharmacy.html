  1. Check your inbox: Look for an email from SaveRx.ai with instructions.

Need help right now?

We’re here to make this easy. If you’re unsure which option fits your situation, start with the coverage you have:

  • Commercial (private) insurance: You may be eligible for a manufacturer copay program.
  • Medicare/Medicaid: Manufacturer copay cards are usually not available; we’ll share other ways to reduce cost.
  • No insurance: We’ll point to pharmacy options and assistance resources.
